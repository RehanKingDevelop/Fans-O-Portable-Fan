How to Use & Charge

🌬️ How to Use

1
Unfold the fan by pulling both sides apart
2
Press the power button to turn on
3
Press again to change speed (Low → Medium → High)
4
Press and hold to turn off
5
Fold back after use for easy storage

🔌 How to Charge

1
Connect USB cable to the charging port on the fan
2
Plug into any USB port — phone charger, power bank, laptop, or car
3
Indicator light shows charging status — red when charging
4
Unplug when fully charged (2-3 hours)
